PIG DICE!

Lets play!

Rules:

    1. If player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player turn. 2. if the player rolls any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues. 3. If the player chooses to hold, their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next players turn. The first player to score 100 or more points wins!
